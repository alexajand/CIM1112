About

Mojo Jojo is an evil ape and supervillain in the Cartoon Network animated series the "Powerpuff Girls." He is the #1 archenemy of the Powerpuff Girls. Mojo was known initially as Jojo, the Professor's lab assistant.

During the creation of the Powerpuff Girls, Jojo broke a container of Chemical X, which resulted in an explosion that caused Jojo's skin to turn green, his brain to enlarge outside of his skull, and the ability to feel new emotions like envy, inferiority, and rage. Mojo Jojo is an extremely accomplished supervillain, chef, and master-manipulator.
