    The Aventura Mall is the fifth-largest shopping mall in the United States, with over 300 stores and 30 resturants.
    Aventura's world-famous Turnberry Golf Course was originally designed by the notable golf course-architect Robert Trent Jones, Sr. and later renovated by golf icon Raymond Floyd.
    Aventura is a hotspot for luxury realtors. "The Point" is one of the newest luxury condominium towers, overlooking the bay.
    The weather in Aventura rarely drops below 70 degrees and sunny- perfect for swimming and outdoor activities.
