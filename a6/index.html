Visit Marrakech

Explore the enchantment of Morocco's "Jewel of the South"

One of Morocco’s historic imperial cities, Marrakesh is often said to be one of the country’s most captivating destinations. Nicknamed the Red City because of the high, reddish-brown walls that enclose the old medina, Marrakesh offers an exciting blend of history, culture, tradition, everyday life, and a unique energy that needs to be experienced to be understood. Add Marrakesh to your bucket list and see why so many people cannot help but be enchanted by the Red City.
